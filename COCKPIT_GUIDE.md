# Cuda Spirit cockpit guide

## First ten minutes

1. Open **Customize** and set region, stage, focus, weekly hours, spending style, budget, and owned conveniences.
2. Link a public adventurer profile when desired.
3. Open **Returner & Reroll Recovery**.
4. Returning account: press **One-click returner rescue**.
5. New account: follow the fresh-character guidance and current season path.
6. Open **Rewards** before opening boxes or consuming coupons.
7. Paste uncertain item tooltips into **Item Intel**.
8. Use **Transfer Lab** before assuming an item is stuck.
9. Score every paid offer in **Pearl Shop Guard**.
10. Use **Navigator** as the only short list of what to do next.

## Returner order

- Recover rewards and account-wide systems.
- Quarantine unknown items instead of cleaning inventory aggressively.
- Check Find My Item, storages, market warehouse, Family Inventory, and character inventories.
- Refresh current patch, class, season, and event information.
- Re-baseline gear and goals.
- Trial classes and current season routes.
- Delay permanent spending and character-bound consumption.

## Before deleting any character

Do not rely on memory. Complete every critical row in the retirement checklist, inspect the live client’s final warning, and prefer buying or earning another character slot over deleting a developed character when uncertainty remains.

## Pearl rules

- A discount is irrelevant when the contents are unwanted.
- Random boxes are valued by expected value, not the jackpot image.
- Character-bound weight, inventory, and outfits belong only on a confirmed long-term main.
- Temporary subscriptions require enough active playtime.
- Duplicate convenience has sharply diminishing value.
- Returning players should use the seven-day no-spend shield.
